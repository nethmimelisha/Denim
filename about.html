Glamovi is a forward-thinking fashion brand redefining denim as a symbol of timeless style, confidence, and innovation. Guided by Directors Kapila Perera and Pasindu Perera, the brand blends strategic excellence with creative artistry to craft premium, durable, and stylish pieces. Every design empowers self-expression while promoting sustainability and responsibility in fashion. Rooted in teamwork and innovation, Glamovi continues to inspire individuals to embrace their true selves. Together, we’re building a global denim community that celebrates individuality and confidence.

OUR VISION

To redefine denim as a symbol of timeless style, comfort, and confidence inspiring individuals
to express their true selves while embracing sustainability and innovation.




OUR MISION

At Glamovi, our mission is to craft high-quality denim that blends modern fashion with everyday functionality.
We are committed to,
• Delivering premium, durable, and stylish pieces.
• Empowering self-expression through innovative designs.
• Practicing sustainability to create fashion with responsibility.
• Building a global denim community that celebrates individuality and confidence.

OUR DIRECTORS



Kapila Perera
Director


Kapila Perera, Director of Glamovi, is a driving force behind the brand’s strategic vision and operational excellence. With years of experience in business management and a strong focus on quality, Kapila has played a pivotal role in shaping Glamovi’s success story. His leadership style emphasizes discipline, teamwork, and innovation—ensuring that every process reflects the brand’s commitment to excellence. Kapila’s guidance continues to steer Glamovi toward sustainable growth and industry recognition.

Pasindu Perera
Director


Pasindu Perera, Director of Glamovi, leads the brand’s creative and stylistic direction with passion and precision. His modern approach to fashion and deep understanding of design trends have helped establish Glamovi as a brand that celebrates individuality and confidence. Pasindu’s vision combines creativity with purpose, inspiring his team to push boundaries and redefine contemporary fashion. Under his leadership, Glamovi continues to evolve as a name synonymous with style, innovation, and elegance.

Sparks X Clothing (Pvt) Ltd.

Sparks X Clothing (Pvt) Ltd. is a dynamic subsidiary of Glamovi Clothing (Pvt) Ltd., operating under the visionary guidance of Mr. Kapila Perera and Mr. Pasindu Perera. Dedicated to innovation and quality, Sparks X focuses on delivering contemporary fashion solutions that blend creativity with precision. The company embodies Glamovi’s core values of discipline, teamwork, and excellence, continuing its mission to redefine modern apparel and empower individuality in every design.
