Glamovi is a forward-thinking fashion brand redefining denim as a symbol of timeless style, confidence, and innovation. Guided by Directors Kapila Perera and Pasindu Perera, the brand blends strategic excellence with creative artistry to craft premium, durable, and stylish pieces. Every design empowers self-expression while promoting sustainability and responsibility in fashion. Rooted in teamwork and innovation, Glamovi continues to inspire individuals to embrace their true selves. Together, we’re building a global denim community that celebrates individuality and confidence.

OUR VISION

To redefine denim as a symbol of timeless style, comfort, and confidence inspiring individuals
to express their true selves while embracing sustainability and innovation.




OUR MISION

At Glamovi, our mission is to craft high-quality denim that blends modern fashion with everyday functionality.
We are committed to,
• Delivering premium, durable, and stylish pieces.
• Empowering self-expression through innovative designs.
• Practicing sustainability to create fashion with responsibility.
• Building a global denim community that celebrates individuality and confidence.
